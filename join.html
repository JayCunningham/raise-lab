Collaborate with RAISE Lab

The RAISE Lab is excited to engage in meaningful collaborations with researchers, practitioners, organizations, and communities who share our commitment to responsible AI development. We believe that the most impactful technology emerges from diverse partnerships that center community voices and prioritize equity.

Our Collaboration Philosophy

We seek partnerships that are mutually beneficial, community-centered, and aligned with our mission of creating AI systems that are fair, culturally competent, and reflective of the values and needs of all people‚Äîespecially those disproportionately affected by technology.
